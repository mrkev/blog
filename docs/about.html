About

Midnight Joke is a blog by Kevin Chavez.

It's called Midnight Joke after a song I like by Takako Mamiya. I made a remix of it and used the soundcloud link to test this blogging platform in one of the first posts. It's also a blog I won't take too seriously, predominantly written past midnight.

It's an experiment in self-publishing, and I guess in static-site generation too.
